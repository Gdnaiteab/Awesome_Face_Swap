##  Datasets
### FFHQ
Introduced by Karras et al. in  [Style-Based Generator Architecture for Generative Adversarial Networks](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.04948v3).

Flickr-Faces-HQ (FFHQ) consists of 70,000 high-quality PNG images at 1024Ã—1024 resolution and contains considerable variation in terms of age, ethnicity and image background. It also has good coverage of accessories such as eyeglasses, sunglasses, hats, etc. The images were crawled from Flickr, thus inheriting all the biases of that website, and automatically aligned and cropped using dlib. Only images under permissive licenses were collected. Various automatic filters were used to prune the set, and finally Amazon Mechanical Turk was used to remove the occasional statues, paintings, or photos of photos.

Source:http:https://github.com/NVlabs/ffhq-dataset
